Projects

A few of the projects I have worked on during the past few years.

Break It

Swift(iOS)

Break It is a casual mobile game for iOS devices. The player rotates a color wheel in the center of the screen while colored balls spawn and move towards the wheel. To progress to more difficult levels, the player must rotate the color wheel so that balls hit and destroy corresponding colors on the wheel.

Antediluvian: Seeds of Gold

Board Game

Antediluvian: Seeds of Gold is a explorative tabletop strategy game. The goal of the game is to enter a temple, collect treasure and avoid traps, and escape before the randomly-triggered flood catches up and causes loss of treasure. The game begins with a few set rooms, but allows players to build in any direction from this preset structure.

Puppy's Pen

Python, Pygame, Gtk

Puppy's Pen is an educational game written in Python using Pygame and Gtk and targeted towards 4th grade students interested in learning how to calculate basic perimeter and area. The game consists of procedurally-generated puzzles which can be solved by drawing rectangles on a grid of increasing size. Each puzzle represents a situation where the owner of a puppy must create a pen so that the puppy has space to romp and frolic outdoors

NanoShot

C#, Unity2D

Nanoshot is a health-themed rail shooter game developed in C# and Unity2D. The player plays as a nano-bot that has been injected into a human bloodstream and must combat disease by shooting bacteria and viruses with pills, while also avoiding attacks from various enemies.

Tectonic Shift

Board Game

Tectonic Shift is a futuristic, turn-based, deck-building, racing board game played on a track made of hexagons. The goal of the game is to outmaneuver your opponents to reach the finish line first. In order to accomplish this, players must use move and turn cards, as well as other actions cards collected during their journey. In addition, players must avoid taking damage from opponents' attacks and collisions with track boundaries to avert mandatory pitstops that cause the loss of a turn. Successful players will need skill and wits, as well as a bit of luck.

Music Video/Lyric Finder

Javascript, HTML5, jQuery, JSON

This is a webpage I built using the Youtube API, a lyric API, and the Spotify API. The user can search for any song and will get back the the music video for the song, the lyrics directly under the video, and the song in a spotify player. The user can login to their spotify account and add the searched for song to a playlist or listen to just the audio.
